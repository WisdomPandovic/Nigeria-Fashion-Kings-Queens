FASHION RUNWAY

By Modupe Mayowa | 01/06/20

When a designer comes out with a new collection or an important Fashion Week comes around, a fashion show is just around the corner. A good understanding of a fashion show is an event put on by a fashion designer to showcase their upcoming line of clothing, in which models walk the catwalk dressed in the clothing created by the designer. There are some gorgeous, skinny (or plus-size) models walking down a runway, with tasteful music in the background. Most fashion designers hire models to fit into their collections. Skinny models are mostly preferred. They show off all the latest creations, wowing us with their glitz and glamour. Fashion shows occurs in all top cities of the world like Paris, New York, Lagos, London, Johannesburg at various times and year.

Runway Models or catwalk models are perhaps the most influential of all models, as they popularize a lot of fashion-driven content and adaptations in personal styling. The height requirements are 5.9 and above for female models and between 5'11" to 6'2" for male models. The fashion industry is very strict and has high, and sometimes unreasonable standards. Since the requirements are not very flexible, it is a hard industry to get into.

Runways Models have alot of influence in the industry, thereby held in high esteem by most outside observers.

Chisom Okeke

Chisom may have already walked the runways aspiring models only dream of, but she still have a long way to go as a model. She has walked for several shows now, such as Gtbankfashionweekend2018 and soon her name would be a house hold name in the industry hopefully. Chisom grew up in Lagos with her family, originally from Imo State and attends University of Lagos. She draws her inspiration from the Model Naomi Campbell. Apart of modelling, she's also a Music promoter and a Brand influencer on instagram. She has less than 100k followers on Instagram but that’s likely to change pronto.
