CELEBRITY STYLE

By Chantale Chinwe | 16/08/20

We live in a world where your musical talent or your voice isn’t good enough to make you the people’s choice. Musicians may get famous for their, well, music, but their senses of style are another iconic part of their personas and performances. Thou fashion and Music works hand in hand, not all Musicians is as smooth in his style as he is in his sound. Runtown the "Mad over you" cronner, always seems to have the greatest style. Runtown is one of the most stylish musician in the Nigeria music industry. He doesn't disappoint. Talent coupled with other factors makes you the artist you’ve always wanted to be and one of the factors is Fashion. Runtown has flawless style inspiration, every combination of his outfit blend so well on him. His personality is revealed through what he wears and how he wears them. His styles are from within, simple and elegant. You just have to love his style sense

Runtown is another top-notch fashionista in the men’s fashion industry, his outlook and charisma speak a lot of his dress sense, and he has an attractive and confident personality. When someone can take different fashion pieces that do not begin to be one group but could adapt each of these individual pieces together to make a fantastic look. A person who dresses well is born and such a person is Runtown.





Runtown was born on 19 August 1989 in Enugu but grew up partly in Abuja and Lagos, where he finished his primary education. After the death of his father, he moved to Abuja with his mother who was working in the Ministry of Health. While growing up, he had the aim of being a professional basketball player. In 2005, he relocated to Enugu after his mother developed an interest in politic. He went on to finish his secondary school education in Enugu State. Runtown is currently studying Fashion Management at a fashion school in New York City.

Every one of us has our fashion signature, it might be a chain, wristwatch, shoe and etc. for some but for Runtown, he cherishes sunglasses. As you can see from most of his outfit, his always on nice looking sunglasses and it fits him perfectly well. Sunglasses are a staple item, no matter what time of year it is. Especially if you're looking to spice up your Fashion Style, sunglasses are here to ensure you'll capture the best kind of selfie in the sunshine.







Chances are that if you're into menswear, you're already following a selection of the most stylish guy on Instagram.
